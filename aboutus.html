Wo we are

We are a consortium of people with growing concerns about the deterioration of society. One area we have studied and will work towards improving is in the area of parenting. Having launched various studies about comparisons of parenting systems spanning six generations, we have comprehensive evidence to suggest that throughout the decades the focus and styles have changed dramatically, in most cases to the detriment of children.
Factors such as the introduction of hand-held devices, the change in parental ratings in the movie industry and the development of graphics and story lines in online gaming platforms, have all led to the decline of what we once called moral standards in today’s society. Our aim is to help anyone involved in raising children through the provision of information to recognize how we all contribute to the characters and attitudes of tomorrows adults. The short cuts we make today will have far reaching results tomorrow and in the years to come, so let’s grow up and start parenting.
